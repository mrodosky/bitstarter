FAQ

What does edud8a build for my school or district?
We build tools that aggregate and analyze all your student data. This results in a view of the "whole student" that is normally very difficult or expensive to obtain. Trends, patterns, and early warnings are easier to identify and at principal and teacher's fingetips at all times. No need to wait for the rare data day to actually see the data used to drive improvement efforts and inform accountability efforts.
What can I do with this data and analysis?
A greater understanding your student and teacher performance data will help schools and districts develop targeted professional development programs. It will help deliver targeted interventions in a timely fashion to all students who need help as well. Many states are imposing significant accountability requirements on schools, this will help monitor progress towards goals, as well as help meet or exceed them. We are building tools to manage these processes and are excited to help educators manage their data driven lives in an easier manner.
